  • Rockhouse Hotel

    Rockhouse is a boutique hotel stretching across the cliffs of Pristine Cove. Nestled amongst eight acres of tropical lush gardens, thatched roofed villas are perched on the water's edge. Guests swim in the sixty foot horizon pool or take the stairs carved into the rock down to the cove for snorkelling on the reef.

    Home to two award winning restaurants. The renowned Rockhouse Spa has a philosophy that is a simple, holistic approach to well-being, health and happiness. Spa treatments are undertaken "on-the-rocks" in the cliff-edge Massage Cabana or at the eight room garden spa. Yoga classes are offered daily overlooking the sea.

  • Accommodations

    Rockhouse has thirty-four air-conditioned rooms providing all the comforts of a boutique hotel in a natural Jamaican environment. Eight Premium Villas, ten Villas, two Garden Villas, four Studios, four Deluxe Standard and six Standard rooms are built from timber, stone and thatch and designed to exist in harmony with the jungle-like garden and volcanic rock of the Hotel's eight acres. All rooms have a private furnished balcony or terrace area.

  • Rooms

    There are six different room styles at the hotel: Standard, Deluxe Standard, Studio, Garden Villa, Villa, and Premium Villas. Amenities include a room safe, a fully stocked mini bar, telephones with overseas direct dialling, luggage rack, hanging space with wood hangers, overhead or oscillating stand fan, and air conditioning. The pressurized water system with master tank storage, and power provision from the back-up generators ensure continuous service. Environmentally conscious, solar hot water is heated throughout the property, with local hot water tank backup for those very rare cloudy days, or unusually high consumption.
